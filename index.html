| Frequently Asked Questions |

how to join ?
Click register button at the top of page and connect with your social media account.
Get Free 5.000 Gold for new members, and invite your friend to get more
how to get the money ?
You can do a work that suit with your ability and rank. after you confirm that the work is done, the money will be transfered to your account.
how do i make a job request ?
You can make a job that you need to assisted with the following criteria:
1. The minimum reward for helpers is 10.000 Gold.
2. The job must be legal and not breaking the rules.
how the job works ?
You make a job request and all of member can see the job. they will contact you to make a negotiation with you.
how the rank works ?
There are five types rank of member: IRON, BRONZE, SILVER, GOLD, and PLATINUM. You will started with IRON rank and you can do a job until the criteria for rank up is complete, you can go up to the BRONZE class after clearing 25 jobs.
